Welcome to Collabospace

Collabospace is a comprehensive workplace collaboration platform designed to help teams work together efficiently. This manual will guide you through all features and functionalities.

Getting Started

Account Creation

The basis of the application begins with account creation. To get started register an account either through direct registration with the application, or use the Google OAuth sign up with your Google Account to avoid manual setup.

Joining & Creating Organisations

In Collbospace organisations are the top level entities, users may join existing organisations or create their own.

Joining an Organisation

To join an existing organisation, you'll need an organisation code from an administrator.

  1. Click on the employee tab on the entry page upon opening the application
  2. Click the "Join Organisation" Button
  3. Enter the Organisation Join Code
  4. The code will be validated and then you can submit to join the organisation
Contact your organisation administrator to obtain the join code.

Creating an Organisation

If you want to create your own organisation, simply switch to the admin tab on the entry page and follow the steps given for creating your own organisation.

Workplace Management

Creating & Managing Workplaces

Once you have either created or joined an organisation, the collaboration begins in workplaces. Workplaces are teams in which employees collaborate on projects through the use of the Collabospace project management suite.

If you have just created an organisation, you must first have at least one employee join your organisation so you can set them as your workplace manager. To create a workplace go to the organisation dashboard and follow the prompts.
